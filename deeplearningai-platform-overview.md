# DeepLearning.AI

**Source:** https://www.deeplearning.ai/
**Founder:** Andrew Ng

## Overview

DeepLearning.AI is an AI education platform founded by Andrew Ng that provides online courses and training for individuals seeking to develop artificial intelligence skills. The platform offers world-class education with hands-on training and a collaborative community of peers and mentors. Over 7 million learners have engaged with their educational content.

## Key Topics

### Course Offerings

**Featured Programs:**

- Generative AI for Everyone
- Machine Learning Specialization
- Data Analytics Professional Certificate
- PyTorch for Deep Learning Professional Certificate
- Agentic AI with Andrew Ng
- Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Build with Andrew (beginner-friendly, 30-minute app creation course)

The platform offers specializations and short courses covering topics from foundational machine learning to advanced agentic systems.
